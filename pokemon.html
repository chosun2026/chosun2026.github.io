Unique Features & Benefits

NEW VERSION
Highlight key features that differentiate the product. Be very skeptical of the original title. "Handmade" should not appear if not in the item description. Never use "Custom" as shorthand for "made-to-order." Only use "Custom" if the buyer can specify their own text, design, name, or dimensions. A product with preset variations is NOT custom. Do not include "digital" if the item is available in physical form.

Etsy Platform Context

NEW VERSION
Craft a title that works well in Etsy search. Prioritize search-relevant terms over evocative or decorative language. Favor concrete, specific descriptors (material, style, technique) over vague adjectives.

Conciseness and Readability

NEW VERSION
The title must be no longer than 12 words. Fewer words is always better when meaning is preserved — if unsure whether to include a word, leave it out. Eliminate redundant words and close synonyms. Every word must add distinct value that no other word already covers.

New Bullet — Comma-Sep. Phrases

NEW
Do not combine comma-separated phrases from the original. Many Etsy titles are lists of keyword phrases (e.g., "Dog Portrait, Dog Wall Art, Dog Gift"). Do not merge them all — that produces a keyword-stuffed result. Identify the single best phrase and build a concise title around it. If the same word appears across multiple phrases, use it exactly once. Never output the original title unchanged.

New Bullet — Tribal / IP

NEW
Tribal/cultural/protected IP: Do not use tribal, indigenous, or ethnicity-specific designations (e.g., "Navajo," "Native American") unless they appear verbatim in the original seller title. These terms carry legal and policy implications and should not be inferred from tags, descriptions, or images.

Final Instruction Paragraph

NEW VERSION
Before outputting, count the words in your title. If the count exceeds 12, remove the least distinctive word and recount. If the new title is longer than the original seller title, stop and rewrite. A rewrite that is longer than the original has failed. When in doubt between two words, always drop the less specific one. Output only the final title — no explanations, no additional text.
