🔵 Qwen ≈ Gemini performance
Qwen (op & np) tracks Gemini closely across all metrics — the only notable gap is a slightly lower heuristic score.
🟠 DPO heuristics: trade-off alert
DPO heuristics models (including dpo_balanced) boost readability, heuristic, and richness scores but at the cost of drastically reduced groundedness. Using Qwen as a base (dpo_heuristics_base_qwen) yields the worst groundedness of all.
🟢 dpo_np & no_cot_fixed — most balanced models
dpo_np and no_cot_fixed both achieve sufficiently high scores across all evaluators without any single metric collapsing — the best overall trade-off.
